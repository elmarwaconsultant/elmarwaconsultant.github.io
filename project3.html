Petroleum & Gas Pipeline Protection
( Aramco & Petroment )

El-Marwa Construction Designs stands at the forefront of engineering consultancy for oil and gas pipeline protection, ensuring that vital energy infrastructure remains uncompromised during large-scale construction and urban development projects.

We are honored to be the official engineering consultant for Petroment, working closely with subcontractors of Aramco Saudi Arabia to provide:

  • Strategic design & risk mitigation plans for petroleum pipelines in expanding urban areas.
  • Specialized engineering solutions to protect high-pressure gas lines during road construction, drainage installations, and industrial projects.
  • Conflict resolution methodologies to prevent structural interference between oil pipelines and sanitation systems.

Key Challenges & Solutions

One of the biggest engineering challenges in new city developments and large-scale infrastructure projects is the intersection of petroleum pipelines with water drainage systems. This issue becomes particularly critical when major roads, tunnels, and urban expansions require excavation near existing pipeline networks.

Our solutions involve:

  • Comprehensive mapping of underground utilities using advanced GIS and survey technologies.
  • Protective encasement & shielding solutions to prevent any structural interference.
  • Reinforcement of oil & gas pipelines with specialized barrier systems.
  • Custom-engineered supports for high-pressure pipelines, ensuring uninterrupted operation during construction activities.
